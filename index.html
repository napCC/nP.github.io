I am a research scientist at Shanghai Artificial Intelligence Laboratory, and adjunct PhD advisor at Shanghai Jiao Tong University. My research interests include generative AI, image and video generation, and diffusion models.

I obtained my dual PhD degree from Shanghai Jiao Tong University and University of Technology Sydney, advised by Xiaokang Yang and Dacheng Tao.

I am always seeking self-motivated PhD students and research interns to join my team. Feel free to contact me if you are interested.
