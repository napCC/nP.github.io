Dr. Xinyuan Chen is a Research Scientist at Shanghai Artificial Intelligence Laboratory, and adjunct PhD advisor at Shanghai Jiao Tong University and Fudan University. She obtained my dual PhD degree from Shanghai Jiao Tong University and University of Technology Sydney, advised by Prof. Xiaokang Yang and Prof. Dacheng Tao.

My research interests lies in the area of computer vision and generative model, including video generation, image generation, and world models.

I am happy to work with self-motivated research interns at Shanghai AI lab. Feel free to contact me if you are interested.
